Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025
Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Cellophane ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Cellophane browser extension and related services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address (when you create an account)
  • Content: Notes, comments, reactions, and media you post through Cellophane
  • Visibility Settings: Your chosen privacy settings for each post

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages where you post content, interaction frequency
  • Browser Information: Browser type and version
  • Device Information: Operating system, device type
  • Analytics Data: Only collected if you consent to cookies

2.3 What We DON'T Collect

  • Browsing history outside of Cellophane interactions
  • Personal data from websites you visit
  • Passwords or payment information from other sites
  • Any data without your explicit consent

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your notes and reactions on websites
  • Sync your content across devices (when logged in)
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Respond to user support requests
  • Detect and prevent abuse or spam

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Public Content

Content marked as "Public" is visible to all Cellophane users visiting the same webpage.

4.2 Private Content

  • "Private" content is only visible to you
  • "Friends" content is visible to users you've connected with
  • "Influencer" content is visible to your followers

4.3 We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your private content without consent
  • Use your data for targeted advertising

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored using industry-standard encryption
  • We use secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security audits are performed
  • You can delete your content at any time

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Withdraw consent for cookies at any time

7. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on consent or legitimate interests
  • Data Portability: You can export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Erasure: Request complete deletion of your data
  • Data Protection Officer: Contact privacy@cellophane.ai

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies only with your explicit consent for:

  • Analytics (Google Analytics) to improve our service
  • Essential functionality (login sessions)
  • User preferences storage

You can manage cookie preferences through the consent banner on our website.

9. Children's Privacy

Cellophane is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the extension or via email.

11. Data Retention

  • Active account data: Retained while account is active
  • Deleted content: Removed within 30 days
  • Inactive accounts: Archived after 12 months of inactivity

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for all international transfers.

13. Contact Information

For privacy concerns or questions, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@cellophane.ai
  • Support: support@cellophane.ai
  • Location: Tel Aviv, Israel

By using Cellophane, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
