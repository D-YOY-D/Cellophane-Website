⚖️Content Moderation & Freedom

Our Approach to Uncensored Expression

Cellophane™ was built to enable free expression on the web. We believe in minimal moderation:

As a general rule, we remove content only when it:

  • Violates applicable laws (CSAM, terrorism, etc.)
  • Contains direct threats of violence
  • Shares private personal information (doxxing)
  • Infringes on intellectual property rights
  • Is commercial spam or malware

As a general rule we do not remove content for these reasons, subject to law and community policy:

  • Political opinions or controversial views
  • Criticism of public figures or companies
  • Adult content (with appropriate visibility settings)
  • Strong language or unpopular opinions
  • Disagreements or debates

User Controls: You can block users, filter content types, and control what you see without affecting others' freedom to express.

🤝Who We Share With

Important: We never sell personally identifiable information. Period.

We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you make cellophanes public or share them
  • Service providers: Supabase (database), Cloudflare (CDN), AWS (hosting) - under data processing agreements and confidentiality obligations
  • Legal requirements: Only when required by valid court order or legal process
  • Safety: To prevent imminent harm or illegal activities
  • Anonymized datasets: Aggregated, de-identified data for research (opt-out available)
  • Business transfers: If Cellophane™ is acquired (users will be notified)

International Data Transfers: We may transfer your data internationally using Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission and UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA) where applicable.

🍪Cookies & Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and local storage for essential functions:

  • Authentication: Session tokens to keep you logged in
  • Preferences: Your UI settings (theme, language, view mode)
  • Draft cellophanes: Temporarily saved locally until posted
  • Security: CSRF tokens for protection
  • Analytics: Basic usage metrics (if you consent)

Cross-site tracking: We do not perform cross-site tracking without explicit consent and as permitted by law.

Local-first approach: Most data stays in your browser until you choose to sync.

In the EEA, for users aged 13–15, we do not perform advertising personalization or profiling without verified parental/guardian consent.

👶Children

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are in the EEA, users aged 13–15 require parental/guardian consent to use the Service; at 16+ no such consent is required. In the UK and other regions, the minimum age is 13. If we learn that we have collected personal data contrary to these rules, we will delete it within a reasonable time. Parents or guardians may contact privacy@cellophane.ai to request restriction or deletion of a child's data.
